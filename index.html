What is Our Product?

Sabeel is a Standalone device, that works with solar energy, with integrated tank to store clean water to be ready anytime anyplace.

A device with multilevel filtration, to clean this type of water especially.

It's fully automated with an indication of when the tank is full and ready to use.

How Our Device Works?

Getting Dirty Water

First of all, our device uses a pump to get a water from the source to start filling the inner tank, this water go through a prelevel filter to remove big substance before filteration begins.

Inner Tank and Coagulant

After filling the inner tank, the device pours alum(aluminum sulfate), as a coagulant, and a motor starts to spin, after 15-20 mins, the water become cleaner and ready to go through the next level of filtration.

Using Real Filters

After 15 mins of using coagulant, we use a pump to get the water from the inner tank to the next stage filter, it starts with small sediments filter to clean up water more and more, and then activated carbon filter, to remove all chemicals, smells and everything.

Saving in the Tank

After this process, the device saves this water in a clean tank, to be ready to use anytime, this tank can help farmer to irregate the land of space feddan once, this process takes 6 hours to fill the tank and be ready to use.
